Chocolate Cake

Preparation Time: 30 minutes

Serving Size: 8 servings

  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 and 3/4 cups flour
  • 3/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water
  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix all the dry ingredients.
  3. Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla extract, mix well.
  4. Stir in boiling water until well combined.
  5. Pour batter into greased pans and bake for 30-35 minutes.
  6. Cool and frost as desired.
